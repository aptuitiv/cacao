Include 'small-screen-button.css' in src/styles/lib/components/navigation/index.css

Also make sure that src/styles/config.css contains the following custom breakpoints.

    @custom-media --c-bp-smallScreenNav (max-width: 1050px);
    @custom-media --c-bp-largeScreenNav (min-width: 1051px);

Those breakpoints are for when the primary navigation is hidden (--c-bp-smallScreenNav) and when it's displayed (--c-bp-largeScreenNav).
